Round 2 — Two-Tier Split, refined

Structure is locked from round 1: dark utility tray (News · Contact, search center, Donate right) over a calmer brand bar carrying the crest and the three primary destinations at 15px. Round 2 tunes exactly three things — trigger density, placeholder copy, and where search lives on mobile. Andrew’s pick (locked): Dense + “Search”.

Trigger density
Three calibration steps. Comfortable is the round-1 control (~37px). Dense (default) fixes the pill at 32px with flatter padding and 14px copy — the whole tray drops to ~48px. Compact runs 28px, drops the / chip, and steps the icon down to keep proportion. All three keep a 44px hit target: an invisible ::after band stays 44px tall, centered on the pill, so the visual gets denser but the touch target never shrinks.
Placeholder copy
“Search the Hall” (default) keeps the institutional voice without round 1’s laundry list; “Search” is the floor. The scope hint — players, teams, classes — belongs inside the omnibox panel where there’s room to teach, not crammed into the trigger.
Mobile: search rides the drawer
The tray doesn’t render at 390 — no persistent search bar. The hamburger drawer opens with a full-width search field pinned at its top (16px input text, so iOS won’t zoom; icon inside the field) and autofocuses it; the three primary rows follow, then News/Contact, with Donate pinned at the foot. Bonus: dropping the mobile bar returns ~50px of chrome and the hanging-crest signature comes back at 390.
Tablet call
Kept a compact persistent trigger in the tray at 768. The tray exists anyway (Donate lives there), there’s ample horizontal room, and permanent search discoverability is the entire point of Two-Tier — folding it into the drawer would leave a vestigial one-button tray. The drawer still carries the search field at tablet (without autofocus), so both routes work.
Flag for device QA
Autofocus pops the software keyboard every time the mobile drawer opens — wired in as requested, but worth a feel-check on hardware. If it gets annoying, drop the autofocus and keep the pinned field; nothing else changes.
Sticky on scroll
Unchanged from round 1: tray only. The slim strip with search and Donate follows the visitor; the brand bar scrolls away with the page. Dense density makes that sticky strip noticeably lighter.

State persists locally (uh-header-refine). Esc closes the drawer and stub; / opens search. Icons are Lucide from CDN with inline SVG fallbacks when offline.
